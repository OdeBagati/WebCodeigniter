Bali Ubud and Kecak Fire Dance is very popular tour package in Bali to visit tourist destination and other interesting place in Ubud Village the center of art and culture in Bali. Ubud is a town in Bali located among rice paddies and steep ravines in the central foothills of the Gianyar Regency. Ubud is one of Bali;s major art and culture center, it has developed to be a large tourism industry. Ubud is perfect tourist destination fot those who love with art and culture history. Tohpati Village

Tohpati Village is famous for Bali’s Batik Center. Batik is a traditional form of painting fabrics. They use dots and lines from wax to decorate the cloth that very impressive to watch. It would be impossible Bali’s batik center to visit or live in Indonesia and not be exposed to one of the country’s most highly developed art forms, Batik.

Tirta Gangga Water Palace

Tirta Gangga Water Park is a park with a large pond, build by the King Karangasem in ancient time which used as a bathing place and recreation for the royal family. The water in this pond is very clear and comes form spring in this place. Along with this tune and development the government made this place as one of the tourist object in the eastern part of Bali. This place is designed so beautiful that it provided a value for attracting tourist to visit this place.

Celuk Village

Celuk Village is famous for the incredible gold and silver handicraft which are available around this area for very low prices. In the past the dwellers of this village were mostly farmers, as tourism was becoming main commodity in Bali more and more villagers left their farm behind and began to learn craft silver and gold. Since 1976 Celuk Village has been popular with its beautiful Jewelry and other silver handicrafts.

Batuan Temple

Batuan Temple is one of the most popular and beautiful village temple which is part of three major temple in a village that is called tri kahyangan. Batuan Temple is designed with Balinese architecture with split gates, stone, guardians, thatched shrines and detailed carving.

Tegalalang Rice Field

Tegalalang Rice Field that designed very beautiful with exquisite hollowing rice field and precisely located on the hill bank. In this place you will see the Balinese farmer di their rice field in oblique area complete with its system irrigation. You will enjoy the beautiful panorama of valley with rice terrace and coconut trees around it.

Mas Village

Mas Village is known as art of wood carving. It is of traditional villages in Bali fundamentally as an artistic countryside by focusing the art of wood carving, Mas Village is famous in the island as Bali wood carving center located in Ubud. It owns the typical style of wood carving by placing forward the distinguish synergy if the humanism and materialism.

Ubud Royal Pallace

Puri Saren Royal Palace, an artistic cultural life center and is an Ubud Kingdom Palace with beautiful balinese traditional housesas a residence of Ubud King. Ubud art market located in front of the palace, all the art and craft sold in this place. You can bring home as a souvenir like slipper, wood idol, bag, clothes, matting, painting, and many others. Lunch will be served in a local restaurant in Ubud.

Ubud Night Market

Before returning to the hotel we will stop at one of night market near Ubud. Balinese people call it “Pasar Senggol”. Here you will find cheap items such as children and adult fashion, beauty care, kitchen utensils, and others. They also offer a wide variety of local food and snacks at very affordable prices. You can also try local food here.
